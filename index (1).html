My Models

I've been modelling since 2019. From there, I made more models, from small die-cast models to a TVS (Television Series) accurate cardboard model. I'll showcase some of those models in this page.

TVS Engines

Thomas

Thomas started in 2019 when I found him in the bottom of my old toy bin. He had the 2009 body with the 2006 face and wheels. Then in 2021, I gave him a white running board and LEGO lamp. I removed that at the beginning of this year. Then, I gave him more faces which you can't see. Last month, I gave him a black base.

Gordon

I started Gordon early this year, when I did some touch ups on his buffers. Then around July, I painted a face for him and made a custom wheelset.

J50 Thomas

This version of Thomas is based off of the original design for Thomas which was a LNER J50. I used a Tomy Rosie that was heavily modified to fit the basis. I chopped off the funnel, and two of the thin domes. Then, my dad used a lighter to melt the the funnel onto the engine. Like before, I used the Apple Barrel paint I had to paint him. I then used a old Percy chassis (from the Sodor Fallout Percy). I repainted it, took a middle wheel from my old Tomy James, and painted it all blue. I masked the edges of the footplate and gave it a coat of black. After that, I started on the faces. I made 3 faces: shocked, happy, and sad. For all of them I referenced the Reginald Dalby illustrations. A few weeks ago, with a lot I got, I replaced the siderods with ones from a Tomy Spencer. As of now, he does not run.
